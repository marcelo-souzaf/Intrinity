# Intrinity

A game by Partypus Games developed for the Game Maker's Toolkit 2023 game jam. [Play on your browser](https://lfzinho.itch.io/intrinity)!

Character classes work similarly to rock paper scissors. Defeat an enemy to take their class and position, but be careful who you attack!

## Controls

Button | Action
--- | ---
`W` or `↑` | Move up
`S` or `↓` | Move down
`A` or `←` | Move left
`D` or `→` | Move right
Left mouse button | Attack
